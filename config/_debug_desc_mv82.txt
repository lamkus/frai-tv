ARTEMIS2 RETRO MOON - Complete restored NASA Apollo 11 moonwalk footage, AI remastered in stunning 8K quality. Over 3 hours of historic first steps on the Moon.

- Scientific experiments and sample collection
- Buzz Aldrin's descent and exploration
- Flag planting ceremony
